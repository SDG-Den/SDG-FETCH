Package Name: sdg-fetch

Source: https://github.com/SDG-Den/SDG-FETCH
Maintainer: SDGDen
Version:0.4

Dependencies:
fastfetch, jp2a, fzf, bat

Description:

and style picker (sdgfetch config) with persistent state.
Commands: sdgfetch (display system info and configurator via subcommands).
